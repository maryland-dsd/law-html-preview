Code of Maryland Regulations

.02 Fee Schedule.

The following fees are established by the Board:

A. Initial application fee:

(1) Dentist . . . $450;

(2) Dental hygienist . . . $275;

B. Limited license fee:

(1) Dentist . . . $225;

(2) Credentials processing (foreign dental school graduate surcharge) . . . $75;

C. Teacher's license fee:

(1) Dentist . . . $225;

(2) Dental hygienist . . . $225;

(3) Credentials processing (foreign school graduate surcharge) . . . $75;

D. Temporary license application fee — dental hygienist . . . $75;

E. Application fee for dentists licensed in another state . . . $450;

F. Application fee for dental hygienists licensed in another state . . . $275;

G. License renewal fee:

(1) Dentist . . . $560;

(2) Dental hygienist . . . $182;

(3) Teacher's license . . . $225;

(4) Dentist late renewal fee (July 1 through July 31 of the year in which the renewal is due) . . . $300;

(5) Dental hygienist late renewal fee (July 1 through July 31 of the year in which the renewal is due) . . . $150;

H. Dental radiation technologist certification fee:

(1) Certification renewal . . . $68;

(2) For the renewal period ending March 1, 2017, a one-time 1-year certificate for dental radiation technologists whose certificates end in an even number . . . $34;

(3) Certification reinstatement . . . $118;

(4) For the period March 2, 2017 through April 1, 2017, for the 2017 renewal period, a late renewal fee for dental radiation technologists whose certificates end in an even number . . . $25;

(5) On and after April 2, 2017, for the 2017 renewal period, a certification reinstatement fee for dental radiation technologists whose certificates end in an even number . . . $59;

(6) For the period March 2, 2018 through April 1, 2018, for the 2018 renewal period, a late renewal fee for dental radiation technologists whose certificates end in an even number . . . $25;

(7) On and after April 2, 2018, for the 2018 renewal period, a certification reinstatement fee for dental radiation technologists whose certificates end in an even number . . . $59;

(8) Late renewal fee (March 2 through April 1 of the year in which the renewal is due) . . . $50;

(9) Initial application fee for applicants holding an active license or certification to practice dental radiation technology in another state . . . $20;

I. Reinstatement of lapsed license fee:

(1) Dentist . . . $860;

(2) Dentist—teacher's license . . . $225;

(3) Dental hygienist . . . $332;

(4) Dental hygienist—teacher's license . . . $225;

J. Lost, destroyed, or duplicate license fee . . . $15;

K. Verification of licensure fee . . . $20;

L. Specialty identification fee (one-time only) . . . $150;

M. Advanced clinical training continuing education program application fee for a dentist who does not hold a Maryland license . . . $25;

N. Dental assistant examination fee . . . $20;

O. Class I and Class II permit fees:

(1) Initial application . . . $1000;

(2) Renewal . . . $300;

(3) Additional location for a like permit — further evaluation not necessary . . . $500;

(4) Additional location for a like permit — further evaluation necessary . . . $700;

(5) Renewal of additional location for a like permit . . . $300;

P. Class III permit fees:

(1) Initial application . . . $1000;

(2) Renewal . . . $600;

(3) Additional location for a like permit — further evaluation not necessary . . . $600;

(4) Additional location for a like permit — further evaluation necessary . . . $700;

(5) Renewal of additional location for a like permit . . . $600;

P-1. Conversion of parenteral sedation administration permit to a Class II permit . . . $300;

P-2. Conversion of a general anesthesia administration permit to a Class III permit . . . $600;

Q. Class III dental school facility permit fee:

(1) Initial application . . . $1000;

(2) Renewal . . . $600;

(3) Additional location permit . . . $600;

(4) Renewal of additional location permit . . . $600;

R. Certificate fee for a treating dentist to allow another dentist or physician to administer anesthesia and sedation at the site of the treating dentist:

(1) Initial application . . . $1000;

(2) Renewal . . . $300;

(3) Additional location certificate . . . $300;

(4) Renewal of additional location certificate . . . $300;

S. Certificate fee for a treating dentist to treat a patient at a practice location other than their own, at which anesthesia and sedation is administered to the patient:

(1) Initial application . . . $1000;

(2) Renewal . . . $300;

(3) Additional location certificate . . . $300;

(4) Renewal of additional location certificate . . . $300;

T. Single fee for dental hygienist recognition as qualified to administer local anesthesia, or qualified to administer nitrous oxide, or qualified to prescribe and administer medication, or any combination thereof . . . $50;

U. Certificate fee for a treating dentist to allow a certified registered nurse anesthetist to administer anesthesia and sedation at a specific practice location:

(1) Initial application . . . $1000;

(2) Renewal . . . $300;

(3) Additional location certificate . . . $300;

(4) Renewal of additional location certificate . . . $300;

V. Inactive status application fee (per renewal):

(1) Dentist . . . $150;

(2) Dental hygienist . . . $75;

W. Board fee for full permit to dispense prescription drugs . . . $75;

X. Required Division of Drug Control inspection fee for full permit to dispense prescription drugs . . . $1,000;

Y. Limited permit for dispensing prescription drugs:

(1) Initial application . . . $75;

(2) Renewal . . . $75;

Z. Application reprocessing fee . . . $50;

AA. Corporate name application fee . . . $150;

BB. Returned check fee . . . $25;

CC. Late fee for failure to notify the Board within 60 days of change of address . . . $10;

DD. Dentists — diskettes, labels, rosters, or tapes . . . $190;

EE. Dental hygienists — diskettes, labels, rosters, or tapes . . . $130;

FF. Dental assistants — diskettes, labels, rosters, or tapes . . . $145;

GG. Jurisprudence Examination . . . $50; and

HH. Copy of Maryland Laws and Regulations on computer disc or booklet . . . $25.

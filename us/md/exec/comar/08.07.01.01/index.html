.01 Definitions.

A. In this chapter, the following terms have the meanings indicated.

B. Terms Defined.

(1) “Commissioned officer” means a forest or park warden, or a forest or park ranger, having the authority stated in Natural Resources Article, §5-206, Annotated Code of Maryland, or a Natural Resources police officer having the authority stated in Natural Resources Article, §1-204, Annotated Code of Maryland.

(2) "Department" means the Department of Natural Resources.

(3) "Developed areas" means those areas within a State forest having permanent facilities and the areas surrounding those facilities.

(4) “Director” means the Director of the Department of Natural Resources — Forest Service, or the Director’s authorized representative.

(5) “Fireworks” means combustible, implosive, or explosive compositions, substances, combinations of substances, or articles that are prepared to produce a visible or audible effect by combustion, explosion, implosion, deflagration, or denotation.

(6) Forest Officer.

(a) "Forest officer" means a commissioned officer.

(b) "Forest officer" includes:

(i) Forest manager;

(ii) Rangers; and

(iii) Other personnel employed by the State to manage and regulate the use of the State forest.

(7) Off-Road Vehicle.

(a) "Off-road vehicle" means a motorized vehicle designed for or capable of cross-country travel on land, water, snow, ice, marsh, swampland, or other natural terrain.

(b) "Off-road vehicle" includes:

(i) A four-wheel drive or low pressure tire vehicle;

(ii) Automobiles;

(iii) Trucks;

(iv) Motorcycles and related two-wheel vehicles;

(v) Amphibious machines;

(vi) Ground effect or air cushion vehicles; and

(vii) Snowmobiles.

(8) “Service” means the Department of Natural Resources — Forest Service.

(9) "State forest road" means a road on or adjacent to the State forest maintained or constructed and maintained by the Service.

(10) "Undeveloped areas" means those areas within a State forest not having permanent facilities.

(11) Vehicle.

(a) "Vehicle" means a mode of transportation by which an individual or property may be transported on a waterway or highway.

(b) "Vehicle" includes:

(i) Vessels;

(ii) Trailers;

(iii) Automobiles;

(iv) Trucks;

(v) Buses;

(vi) Mopeds;

(vii) Animals;

(viii) Animal-drawn vehicles; and

(ix) Bicycles.

(12) Vessel.

(a) "Vessel" means any type of watercraft, other than a seaplane, used or capable of being used as a means of transportation on water or ice.

(b) "Vessel" includes:

(i) The vessel's motor, spars, sails, and accessories; and

(ii) Ice boats.

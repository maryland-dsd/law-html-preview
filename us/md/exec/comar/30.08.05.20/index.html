.20 Trauma Program Manager.

PARC I II III ED
A. There shall be a Trauma Program Manager (TPM) who is a registered nurse and who is responsible for the organization of services and systems necessary for a multidisciplinary approach to providing care to the injured patient in collaboration and conjunction with the Trauma Medical Director (TMD). B. There shall be a defined organizational structure which delineates the roles and responsibilities of the TPM. E E E E NA
C. This must be a full-time (1.0 FTE) position dedicated to the management of the trauma program. NA E E E NA

.01 Susquehanna River.

The Susquehanna River encompasses all the waters of the Susquehanna River and its tributaries north of a line beginning at a point, at or near Lat. 39° 32.367' N., Long. 76° 5.0830' W., then running 60° (True) to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 32.850' N., Long. 76° 4.000' W.

.02 Lower Susquehanna River.

The Lower Susquehanna River encompasses the area south of a line beginning at the east end of the old railroad bridge, at or near Lat. 39° 34.323' N., Long. 76° 4.822' W., then running 242° (True) to the east shore of Garrett Island, at or near Lat. 39° 34.243' N., Long. 76° 5.013' W., east of a line running southerly along the shore to the south end of Garrett Island, at or near Lat. 39° 33.552' N., Long. 76° 5.107' W., then north and east of a line running 178° (True) to the AMTRAK railroad bridge, at or near Lat. 39° 33.285' N., Long. 76° 5.095' W., then running 69° (True) to the east end of the AMTRAK railroad bridge, at or near Lat. 39° 33.382' N., Long. 76° 4.775' W. This area has a 6-knot speed limit all year.

.03 Broad Creek.

Broad Creek encompasses the area upstream of a line beginning at a point, at or near Lat. 39° 41.992' N., Long. 76° 14.280' W., then running 164° (True) to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 41.877' N., Long. 76° 14.237' W., and running to the head of the creek. This area has a 6-knot speed limit all year.

.04 Conowingo Creek.

Conowingo Creek encompasses the area upstream of a line beginning at a point, at or near Lat. 39° 40.987' N., Long. 76° 11.857' W., then running 137° (True) to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 40.932' N., Long. 76° 11.790' W., and running to the head of the creek. This area has a 6-knot speed limit all year.

.05 Conowingo Dam — Prohibited Area.

Conowingo Dam encompasses the area north of a line beginning at point, at or near Lat. 39° 39.762' N., Long. 76° 9.605' W., then running 227° (True) to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 39.245' N., Long. 76° 10.320' W., and south of a line beginning at a point, at or near Lat. 39° 40.197' N., Long. 76° 10.483' W., then running 199° (True) to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 39.438' N., Long. 76° 10.812' W. Vessels are prohibited in this area all year.

.06 Havre de Grace.

A. Area. Excluding the area defined in Regulation .07 of this chapter, the area encompasses all of the waters enclosed by a line beginning at a point at or near Lat. 39° 32.262' N., Long. 76° 5.541' W., then running 176° to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 32.113' N., Long. 76° 5.526' W., then running along the North shore of Tydings Island Eastward to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 32.193' N., Long. 76° 5.31' W., then running 93° to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 32.177' N., Long. 76° 4.902' W., then running 2° to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 32.502' N., Long. 76° 4.888' W., then running 355° to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 32.742' N., Long. 76° 4.913' W., then running 338° to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 32.87' N., Long. 76° 4.98' W., then running 328° to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 33.226' N., Long. 76° 5.273' W., then running 252° to a point, at or near Lat. 39° 33.185' N., Long. 76° 5.438' W., then running South along shore to the point of beginning.

B. The speed limit for the area described in §A of this regulation is 6 knots on Saturdays, Sundays, and State holidays during the boating season.

.07 Susquehanna River Rail Bridge Project.

A. The construction area encompasses all of the waters of the Susquehanna River enclosed by a line beginning at a point defined by Lat. 39°32.969' N, Long. 76°5.268' W; then running 329° True to a point defined by Lat. 39°33.509' N, Long. 76°5.682' W; then running 60° True to a point defined by Lat. 39°33.927' N, Long. 76°4.762' W; then running 168° True to a point defined by Lat. 39°33.178' N, Long. 76°4.548' W; then running 249° True to the point of beginning.

B. The speed limit for the area described in this regulation is 6 knots at all times.

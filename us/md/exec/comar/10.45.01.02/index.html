.02 Definitions.

A. In this subtitle, the following terms have the meanings indicated.

B. Terms Defined.

(1) "Applicant" means a person that submits to the Commission, within the time period specified by the Commission:

(a) A letter of intent to submit a grant application under this subtitle; or

(b) A grant application under this subtitle.

(2) "Application" means a document:

(a) Prepared by an applicant in response to the Commission's call for proposals; and

(b) That includes the elements specified in COMAR 10.45.06.03.

(3) "Call for proposals" means a document issued by the Commission that:

(a) Invites members of the public to develop and submit proposals for projects and programs for which grant funding under this subtitle is sought;

(b) Specifies the required content of a grant application and proposal; and

(c) Identifies selection criteria and funding priorities to be applied by the Commission in determining which proposals will be funded.

(4) "Commission" means the Maryland Community Health Resources Commission.

(5) "Commissioner" means a member of the Commission.

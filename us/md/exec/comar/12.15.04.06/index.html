Code of Maryland Regulations

.06 Application Processing.

A. An applicant shall submit the request to the Central Repository.

B. The Central Repository shall accept an applicant's properly completed fingerprints and application and the Central Repository shall:

(1) Conduct the State CHRI check;

(2) Forward the appropriate information and fee to the FBI to conduct the national CHRI check;

(3) Receive notice from the FBI confirming receipt of the request made to the FBI to conduct a national CHRI check;

(4) Prepare a printed statement of the applicant's State CHRI; and

(5) According to Criminal Procedures Article, §§10-201—10-234, Annotated Code of Maryland, forward the printed statement prepared under §B(4) of this regulation to the applicant.

.08 Charter Boat/Fishing Guide Point Assignment Schedule.

A. For each conviction of a chargeable violation listed in §§D—J of this regulation, the Department shall enter on the record of a fishing guide the number of points listed for that particular offense. Points accumulated under this regulation only apply to the entitlement to guide fishing parties or suspension or revocation of a tidal fish license or authorization to guide fishing parties.

B. The number of points specified in §§D—J of this regulation shall be assessed against a fishing guide for each conviction.

C. Points will only be assessed for violations that occurred during a guided fishing trip, unless otherwise noted in the table below by the phrase “points assessed for all violations”.

K. In addition to any other applicable penalty, a person may be suspended for a period of not more than 3 years for an offense related to a violation of the Lacey Act, 16 U.S.C. §§3371—3378, including but not limited to conspiracy to commit a violation of the Lacey Act, 18 U.S.C. §371.

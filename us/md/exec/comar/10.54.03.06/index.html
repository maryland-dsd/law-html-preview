.06 Food Packages for Peer Group Price Calculation.

A. The State agency shall group the food items on the vendor price list form into the following food packages:

(1) Foods for an infant 4 months old or older but younger than 13 months old:

(a) 31 13-fluid-ounce cans of concentrated liquid infant formula with iron as specified in the WIC infant formula rebate contract;

(b) 24 ounces of dry infant cereal; and

(c) 128 ounces of infant vegetables and fruit;

(2) Foods for a child 1 year old or older but younger than 5 years old:

(a) 3 gallons and 1 quart of fluid milk;

(b) 1 dozen large eggs;

(c) 36 ounces of dry cereal;

(d) 128 fluid ounces of single-strength canned juice;

(e) 18 ounces of peanut butter;

(f) 1 pound of cheese; and

(g) 2 pounds of whole wheat bread, brown rice, or soft corn tortillas;

(3) Foods for a breastfeeding woman:

(a) 5 gallons and 1 quart of fluid milk;

(b) 2 dozen large eggs;

(c) 36 ounces of dry cereal;

(d) 36 ounces frozen juice concentrate;

(e) 18 ounces of peanut butter;

(f) 2 pounds of cheese;

(g) 30 ounces of tuna, salmon, or sardines; and

(h) 16 ounces of dried beans; and

(4) Foods for a nonbreastfeeding, postpartum woman:

(a) 3 gallons and 1 quart of fluid milk;

(b) 1 dozen large eggs;

(c) 36 ounces of dry cereal;

(d) 24 fluid ounces of single-strength frozen juice concentrate; and

(e) 1 pound of cheese.

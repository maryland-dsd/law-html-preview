Subtitle 40 LOCKSMITHS

Chapter 01 Fees

Administrative History

Effective date: June 25, 2012 (39:12 Md. R. 745)

Authority

Business Regulation Article, §§12.5-104, 12.5-202, 12.5-209, and 12.5-210, Annotated Code of Maryland

.01 Purpose.

This chapter is intended to carry out the provisions of Business Regulation Article, §§12.5.-202, 12.5-209 and 12.5-210, Annotated Code of Maryland. In particular, it is intended to set various fees associated with licensing of locksmiths.

.02 Schedule of Fees.

Fees charged by the Secretary are as follows:

A. Application Fee — $225;

B. License Renewal Fee — $225; and

C. Change of Name Fee — $ 50.

Chapter 02 General Regulations

Administrative History

Effective date: December 31, 2018 (45:26 Md. R. 1241)

Authority

Business Regulation Article, §§12.5-104, 12.5-209(g), and 12.5-211(a)(1)(xiii), Annotated Code of Maryland

.01 Failure to Respond.

A. Except as otherwise provided in Business Regulation Article, §12.5-303, Annotated Code of Maryland, if an applicant or licensee receives from the Secretary or the Secretary’s designee a written communication requesting a response, the applicant or licensee shall respond in writing within 30 days of the date of the mailing.

B. The Secretary or the Secretary’s designee shall send a written communication by first-class mail to the last business address furnished to the Secretary or the Secretary’s designee by the applicant or licensee.

C. It is the responsibility of an applicant or licensee to notify the Secretary or the Secretary’s designee in writing if there has been a change in an applicant’s or licensee’s address.

D. Failure to respond as required by this regulation may be considered by the Secretary or the Secretary’s designee to be a violation of Business Regulation Article, §12.5-211(a)(1)(xiii), Annotated Code of Maryland.

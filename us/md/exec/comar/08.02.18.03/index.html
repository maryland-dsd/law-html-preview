.03 Freshwater Fishing Guide License — Type NT.

A. A type NT freshwater fishing guide license, as authorized by Natural Resources Article, §4-210.1, Annotated Code of Maryland, authorizes the licensee to provide fishing guide services only in the nontidal waters of the State.

B. Striped Bass. An angler under the guidance of a licensee with a valid type NT license in nontidal waters of the State may:

(1) Hook striped bass and immediately release the striped bass unharmed to the water from which the striped bass was taken; and

(2) Harvest striped bass from the following areas:

(a) Youghiogheny Reservoir;

(b) Piney Run Reservoir;

(c) Tridelphia Reservoir;

(d) Loch Raven Reservoir;

(e) Prettyboy Reservoir;

(f) T. Howard Duckett (Rocky Gorge) Reservoir;

(g) Liberty Reservoir; and

(h) Conowingo Reservoir.

C. Fees. The fees for a type NT license are:

(1) For a resident, $20; and

(2) For a nonresident, $50.

D. General. Except in Deep Creek Lake, the maximum number of individuals present on a vessel which is operated by a licensee may not exceed four, including the licensee.

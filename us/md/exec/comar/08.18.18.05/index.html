.05 Ocean Pines Canals, Creeks, Channels, Basins, and Inlets.

A. The Ocean Pines canals, creeks, channels, basins, and inlets encompass those areas of Manklin Creek and St. Martin River bordering the community of Ocean Pines as set forth in §§B—D of this regulation.

The area north and west of a line beginning at the west side of Wood Duck canal, at or near Lat. 38° 22.383’ N., Long. 75° 8.500’ W., then running 93° (True) to the east side of Wood Duck canal, at or near Lat. 38° 22.380’ N., Long. 75° 8.418’ W., then running easterly along the shore to the west side of Goldeneye canal, at or near Lat. 38° 22.357’ N., Long. 75° 8.358’ W., then running 78° (True) to the east side of Goldeneye canal, at or near Lat. 38° 22.372’ N., Long. 75° 8.265’ W., then running easterly along the shore to the west side of a cove, at or near Lat. 38° 22.378’ N., Long. 75° 8.202’ W., then running 77° (True) to the east side of the cove, at or near Lat. 38° 22.382’ N., Long. 75° 8.183’ W., then running easterly along the shore to the west side of Wood Duck canal, at or near Lat. 38° 22.373’ N., Long. 75° 8.128’ W., then running 97° (True) to the west end of an island, at or near Lat. 38° 22.363’ N., Long. 75° 8.032’ W., then running easterly along the shore to the east end of the island, at or near Lat. 38° 22.388’ N., Long. 75° 7.980’ W., then running 356° (True) to the shore at Wood Duck Drive, at or near Lat. 38° 22.407’ N., Long. 75° 7.982’ W., then running D. Marina Village Basin encompasses all of the area upstream and westerly of a line beginning at a point, at or near Lat. 38° 22.737’ N., Long. 75° 7.407’ W., then running 4° (True) to Ocean Pines Yacht Club Light 2, at or near Lat. 38° 22.973’ N., Long. 75° 7.385’ W., then running 3° (True) to a point, at or near Lat. 38° 23.076’ N., Long. 75° 7.378’ W., then running 319° (True) to a point on shore, at or near Lat. 38° 23.217’ N., Long. 75° 7.532’ W. This area has a 6-knot speed limit all year.

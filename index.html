Code of Maryland Regulations

The Code of Maryland Regulations (COMAR) contains all regulations for Maryland State agencies and departments. There is no cost to use COMAR online.

COMAR is updated every two weeks.

Learn about the COMAR numbering system.

Maryland Register

Before a regulation is adopted and incorporated into COMAR, it is available in a proposed form in the Maryland Register for the public to review and provide feedback on. The Register is published every two weeks and includes:

  • Governor's Executive Orders
  • General Assembly Synopses
  • Judiciary Notices and Opinions
  • Division of State Documents
  • Open Meetings and Compliance Board
  • Changes to Maryland Regulations
  • Regulatory Review and Evaluation
  • Agency Meeting and Hearing Notices
  • Other documents considered to be in the public interest
